SON OF TROX: CRIMSON FALL

“Tell him I chose him. Every single day, I would choose him.”

A story-driven 2D Action RPG built in Godot 4.


Overview

SON OF TROX: CRIMSON FALL follows Axle, the son of the legendary warrior Trox.

Raised in the hidden village of Nivedj after his father’s mysterious disappearance, Axle grows up carrying a legacy he never asked for and a bloodline he does not fully understand.

What begins as a story about grief and loss slowly becomes a larger conflict surrounding power, inheritance, betrayal, and the cost of legacy.

The story revolves around one central question:

“Was it worth what it cost?”


The World

The world of SON OF TROX is shaped by the legacy of Trox — a warrior feared by kings, admired by the people, and deeply connected to the natural world.

Ten years after his disappearance, rumours of his death still haunt the land.

Hidden villages, ancient forests, sacred bloodlines, forgotten truths, and a stolen sword all become part of Axle’s journey as he searches for answers about his father and himself.


Main Characters

Axle — The Unwilling Heir  
An 18-year-old driven by grief, confusion, and the need to understand what happened to his father.

Trox — The Legend  
The greatest warrior the land had ever known. Missing for ten years. Presumed dead.

The Uncle — The Betrayer Within  
A man consumed by resentment toward Trox and the bloodline he believed took everything from him.

The Guru — The Last Connection to Trox  
An old master hidden deep within the forest who once trained Trox himself.

The Gang Leader — The Final Question  
The man connected to Trox’s disappearance and the holder of the sacred sword.


Story Structure

Chapter 1 — Introduction  
Axle lives peacefully in Nivedj before betrayal destroys everything he knows.

Chapter 2 — Et tu, Brute  
The truth behind the uncle’s betrayal unfolds while Axle begins training with the guru.

Chapter 3 — Root Awakening  
Axle travels beyond the forest and begins understanding the true nature of the bloodline.

Chapter 4 — The Mystery Unveiled  
The truth surrounding Trox’s disappearance is finally revealed.

Chapter 5 — Priced Possession  
Axle reaches the sacred sword and faces the cost of claiming it.

Chapter 6 — Bloodwar  
Every thread converges in the final confrontation surrounding legacy, power, and choice.
